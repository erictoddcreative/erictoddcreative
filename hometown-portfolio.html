Hometown Heroes Wallpapers

These phone wallpapers were created as a personal project to sharpen my skills in sports design. I chose to create wallpapers of players that grew up in my home state of North Carolina and have since moved to big cities to play pro sports. I wanted to practice with texture and lighting effects which are supremely important in sports marketing design.

The main focus of this project was to create a solid composition with the player in the narrow format that phone wallpapers require. I did a lot of work testing different lighting effects with the bright whites to symbolize the bright lights of the large cities they play in. I also focused on being quick with this project, because in the world of sports designers are called upon to create graphics for unexpected events right after they happen (like a player having a hat-trick in a game).
