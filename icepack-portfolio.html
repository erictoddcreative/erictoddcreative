Icepack Hockey

This is the logo design I created for the NC State Club Hockey team to use for their team branding and advertising. Previously, they had been dealing with trademarks issues from using the NC State logo, since they are just a club team. Being a member of the team and the Head of Social Media, I took initiative to solve this problem and craft a brand for the team to use for years to come. I created this logo that will be printed on new jerseys, merchandise, and used across all marketing and advertising mediums for the team.

This image includes all iterations of the standard wolf head logo that should be used in materials. It's main purpose is to specify how to properly flip between a light and dark monotone version of the logo.

This image includes the two iterations of the throwback logo that should be used in materials. This logo was chosen to be used on the third jersey that was created for the 2019-2020 season.

Website Redesign

The new site design focused on the new generation of NC State Club hockey, rebranded as Icepack Hockey. I chose to use Weebly for this redesign because it is very simple to use and update, which will be beneficial to the team after I graduate and no longer update it. A main goal of mine was to keep the website as timeless as possible, which was not considered in creating the previous site. I used black, greys, and white with very minimal amounts of graphical elements to accomplish this goal. I also focused on streamlining the old website, which featured a nightmare of a navigation bar with 8 main tabs and tons of drop-downs. I simplified all of these pages as best I could, categorizing everything by what people are looking to do on the site.
