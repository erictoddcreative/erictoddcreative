Anna's Bakery Brand Design

This case study originated from a prompt given to me for a logo design, the prompt was to create a cupcake logo of any kind. Naturally, I decided this logo would be for a high end bakery targeting upper-middle to upper class middle-aged adults that wanted to treat themselves to a cupcake, or place a custom order for their next big event. I came up with the name Anna's Bakery (formerly Anna's Cupcakes) because using the name of the owner/baker in the store helps connect to the community on a personal level which is important for a small business.

The logo was created in a hand-drawn style because this enhances the homemade aspect of the company. Individuality and creativity is very important in this company that makes custom desserts, so the logo should represent that. The pink background is a similar shape to how icing looks from the top view of a cupcake, and gives the mark character and color. The typography is also following the hand-drawn style, and a thin script font fits the target market of upper-middle to upper class because script fonts are typically associated with luxury.

The Website

The website design was saving customer time in mind. There are three links in the navigation that are the three things customers most often visit restaurant websites for. The design of the site follows the hand-drawn style of the logo which adds personality and solidifies the brand in the customer's mind.
